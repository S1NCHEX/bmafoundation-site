Our Story

Founded in 2025 by aspiring doctors at Tbilisi State Medical University, BMA Foundation was born out of the belief that medicine is more than science — it's service.

We've turned creativity into action — raising awareness and resources for real people facing real needs.

Every decision we make is grounded in transparency, fueled by student energy, and aimed at long-term community upliftment.

Our Core Values

Transparency

Every donation is publicly accounted for — always.

Creativity

address social, medical, and emotional challenges through creative action.

Community

We collaborate with local voices and uplift together.
